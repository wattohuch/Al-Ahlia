About Us

Our Story

Established in 1978, Al Ahlia Contracting Group (ACG) has been shaping Kuwait's skyline with its industry-leading expertise in construction and engineering.

Our services cover construction strategies, management, architectural detailing, cost planning, and value engineering. We're proud to be a Category I registered contractor with Kuwait's Central Tendering Committee.

With a team of highly skilled professionals and a commitment to quality, we've successfully delivered numerous landmark projects that stand as testaments to our capabilities.
