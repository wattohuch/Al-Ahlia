Our Story

Established in 1978, Al Ahlia Contracting Group (ACG) has been a cornerstone of Kuwait's contracting business for over four decades. We are committed to providing the highest quality service in our comprehensive range of services, from initial construction strategies to final project delivery.

Our aim is to build lasting relationships with our clients by utilizing proven, innovative techniques and maintaining stringent control over timing, cost, and quality. We strive to complete every project on time, on budget, and beyond expectations.
