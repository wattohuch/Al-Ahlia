About Our Company

Discover the legacy of excellence that defines our carpentry work

Excellence in Carpentry Since 1995

Established in 1995, AACG specializes in bespoke carpentry and wooden decoration. Over the years, we have executed numerous luxurious projects under the administration of specialized personnel with decades of experience in the field.

Our team comprises 13 dedicated employees with a combined working experience of over 198 years. We are proud to provide a wide range of the highest quality materials, sourced directly from the Kuwaiti market or imported from abroad.
