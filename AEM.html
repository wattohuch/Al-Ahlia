About Us

Quality and expertise at the forefront of electro-mechanical solutions

Leading Electro-Mechanical Solutions Provider

Al Ahlia Electro-Mechanical Group started its operations in 1985, specializing in all electrical & telephone installations as well as plumbing and fire-fighting works. We have completed many projects in both public and private sectors.

Our company is run by a technical team of specialist engineers with many years of experience in the sector. We enjoy an outstanding reputation for quality performance, expertise, and reliability in all our electro-mechanical solutions.

Quality is an integral part of our company strategy, lying at the top of our working priorities. Our quality assurance program is implemented through rigorous inspections at predetermined manufacturing stages and comprehensive on-site installation checks.
